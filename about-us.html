About Us

United Structural Systems of Illinois, Inc

United Structural Systems of Illinois, Inc - Company Description United Structural Systems of Illinois, Inc (USS) is the premier authority in professional crawlspace waterproofing, foundation repair, and structural stabilization across the Greater Chicago area. For decades, our team has specialized in protecting residential and commercial properties from the unique geological and climatic challenges of the Midwest. Based in Hoffman Estates, we provide permanent, engineered solutions that address the root causes of moisture intrusion, structural settling, and indoor air quality degradation. Our comprehensive crawlspace waterproofing and encapsulation services are designed to transform damp, neglected spaces into dry, healthy environments. We utilize industrial-grade vapor barriers (20-mil thickness), high-efficiency dehumidification systems, and advanced drainage solutions—including sump pump installations and French drains—to prevent mold growth and wood rot. By sealing off the earth’s natural moisture and soil gases, we help homeowners in Wheaton, Naperville, Elmhurst, and the surrounding suburbs significantly reduce energy costs while protecting their building’s structural integrity. Beyond waterproofing, United Structural Systems is a leader in foundation piering and pressure grouting. We utilize high-strength steel push piers and helical piers to stabilize foundations experiencing settlement or shifting. Our expertise extends to basement wall reinforcement, using carbon fiber straps or wall anchors to remediate bowing or cracked basement walls caused by hydrostatic pressure. At USS, we pride ourselves on a "fix it once" philosophy. We don't believe in temporary patches; we believe in permanent repairs backed by industry-leading warranties. Every project begins with a detailed inspection to identify specific site conditions, such as high water tables or expansive clay soils. Whether you are dealing with a damp crawlspace in Bartlett or a cracked foundation in Hoffman Estates, United Structural Systems of Illinois, Inc delivers the technical expertise and honest service required to protect your most valuable asset.

        What is the purpose of installing a vapor barrier in a crawlspace in Wheaton, Illinois?
        The purpose of installing a vapor barrier in a crawlspace is to prevent moisture from the ground from entering the crawlspace, which can lead to mold growth, structural damage, and decreased energy efficiency.         What materials are typically used for vapor barriers in crawlspace waterproofing?
        Vapor barriers are typically made from durable polyethylene plastic sheeting. The thickness can vary, but a 6 to 20 mil thickness is commonly used for effective moisture control. In some cases, reinforced vapor barriers are used for added durability.
